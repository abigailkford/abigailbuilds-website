Usage Guidelines

Deep Teal (#0F5F5C): Primary brand color for headers, buttons, CTAs, and key navigation elements.

Soft Mint (#D9F3EE): Backgrounds, subtle accents, and section dividers. Creates breathing room.

Golden Apricot (#F4A259): Accent color for highlights, hover states, important labels, and visual interest.

Off-White (#FAF9F6): Main backgrounds and light surfaces. More elegant than pure white.
